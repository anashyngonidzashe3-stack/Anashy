Academic Programs

Secondary School Forms

We currently offer secondary education from Form 1 through Form 3, with enrollment open for the 2026 academic year.

01
Form One
Open for 2026

The entry point into secondary education at Focus Group. Students build foundational skills across core subjects in a nurturing, structured environment designed to ease the transition from primary school.

02
Form Two
Open for 2026

Building on Form One fundamentals, students deepen their subject knowledge. Talent identification, counseling, and extra-curricular activities play an increasing role in student development.

03
Form Three
Open for 2026

Form 3 prepares students for the critical O-Level examinations ahead. With highly qualified tutors and our Technical Skills Hub, students are set up for examination success and beyond.
